Our story

Built by a family,
for families.

Our vision

To be a transformative force in our communities — making a meaningful impact on the lives of homeowners and fostering a legacy of service, connection, and pride that resonates for generations.

Our mission

To provide a safe haven for homeowners by helping them repair, rebuild, and refurbish their most cherished spaces. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and craftsmanship — ensuring every project enhances the comfort and security of the home.

The founders

Lauren & Mario Merrills

Mario Merrills

Co-Founder

A native of Howard County, Maryland, Mario was a standout football and track athlete at Wilde Lake High School before earning a football scholarship to the University of Maryland. After a career in medical device sales, he founded Harbour to deepen his impact on the community he grew up in — turning a builder's eye and an athlete's discipline toward the homes of his neighbors.

Lauren Merrills

Co-Founder

Originally from Cincinnati, Ohio, Lauren earned a soccer scholarship to Ohio State University and built her own career in medical device sales. At Harbour she leads operational excellence and the quality of the client experience — the through-line that makes every project feel as considered as it is well-built.

Family first

Lauren and Mario are raising four children — Milan, Reese, Maverick, and Camden — on values of community service, athletics, and local ownership. The same care they bring home, they bring to yours.
