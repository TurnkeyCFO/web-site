How do recurring service plans work on the website?

We build a dedicated recurring-plans section that explains your monthly, quarterly, or seasonal options in clear, simple terms. Visitors can request to sign up directly from the page — no phone tag required. Recurring accounts are the most valuable thing a window cleaning business can have, and the website should be actively selling them.

Yes, and this is often the biggest opportunity for window cleaning companies. We build a separate commercial section tailored to property managers and business owners — different pain points, different language, different call to action.
