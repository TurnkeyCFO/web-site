FOUR DECADES UNDER THE HOOD.

Twisted Garage has been an Ocala fixture for 40 years, run hands-on by owner Mike. Most jobs in this business bounce between a mechanic, a body shop and a painter. Here it stays in one building — torn down, machined, fabricated, painted and put back together by the same crew who started it.

That's the reason customers keep coming back: real diagnosis, real craftsmanship, and an owner who still returns the keys himself. As one reviewer put it, "this shop kinda does it all."
