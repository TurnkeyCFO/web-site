Our process

Inside one repair

A bad scrape down the side of a customer’s car. Mostly clearcoat damage, with a slight crease. Here is every stage of it, start to finish.

Step 01

A really bad scrape, slight crease, mostly damage to the clearcoat.

Step 02

Dents pulled, filler applied and left to dry.

Step 03

Sanded and primed, ready to paint.

Step 04

Painted, then wet sanded and buffed.

Step 05

Polished. Very shiny, and unnoticeable to the casual observer.
