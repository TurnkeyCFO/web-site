What We Do

Everything outside, handled by one crew.

From a single overgrown oak to a full yard makeover — tell us what's bothering you and we'll tell you straight what it takes. Free estimate before any work begins.

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Shaping, thinning, and deadwood removal that keeps your trees healthy and your house clear of low limbs. We trim oaks the right way — for the tree, not just the look.

Tree Removal

Dead, leaning, storm-damaged, or just in the wrong place — we take it down safely, in tight spaces, and clean up like we were never there. Emergency and storm cleanup too.

Stump Grinding

Ground down below the surface so you get your yard back — no tripping hazard, no regrowth, no eyesore. We haul the grindings or leave them for mulch, your call.

Lawn Care & Mowing

Regular mowing, edging, weed removal, mulch, and sod. A clean, even lawn every week — the kind that makes the whole street look better.

Drainage & Sprinklers

Standing water after every Houston rain? We install and repair drainage systems and sprinklers so your yard sheds water instead of holding it.

Landscaping & Yard Projects

Beds, fences, gravel, sod, and full yard makeovers. If it's outside the house, the same crew that knows your trees can plan and build the rest.

Our Story

Thirty-four years, one neighbor at a time.

Mr. Garcia started this business in Katy more than three decades ago, and the way he runs it hasn't changed: show up, do the job right, and let the work earn the next call. No call center, no upselling — just a family crew that has cleared, trimmed, and cared for yards across Katy and west Houston for 34 years and counting.

Most of our work still comes the same way it always has — a neighbor sees the yard next door and asks who did it. That word-of-mouth reputation is the whole company. We'd rather keep one customer for twenty years than chase a hundred for one.
