Meet your coach

I'm Kendra. I help women stop quitting on themselves.

I've been a personal trainer since 2013 and a virtual coach since 2020. In 2025, after a season that reshaped everything I thought I knew about wellness, I rebranded my coaching practice as EmpowerHer Health Coaching and added Health Coach certification to my toolkit — because training is only one piece of the picture.

I work with women who've been on the diet–binge–start-over loop for years. We replace that loop with something quieter: a strength plan you can actually do, a nutrition rhythm you don't have to white-knuckle, and a coach who isn't going to disappear when life gets messy.

  • Personalized strength training built around your schedule, equipment, and body
  • Nutrition guidance that fits real life — no rigid meal plans, no cutting out food groups
  • Sleep, stress, and habit coaching, because the body doesn't change in a vacuum
  • Direct accountability — I'm in your corner every week, not just on Mondays

FAQ

Common questions.

I've never lifted before. Am I going to be lost?
No. Most of my 1:1 clients start with zero strength training background. Every lift gets a video demo, and your program starts where you are — not where Instagram fitness culture says you should be.
Do I need a gym?
No. I build for whatever you've got — gym, garage setup, or just dumbbells and a bench. Real life equipment is part of the assessment.
What if I'm in a different time zone?
Virtual coaching is the whole point. I have clients across the U.S. and internationally — check-ins are asynchronous, calls are scheduled around your time zone.
How is this different from a meal plan or a workout app?
A meal plan tells you what to eat for 30 days. An app gives you a generic program. I give you a personalized strategy that adapts as you do — and a coach who notices when you're stuck before you quit.
What's the commitment?
1:1 coaching is a 3-month minimum (because real change takes longer than 30 days), then month-to-month. We discuss everything on the free consult.
How do I get started?
Tap any "Message Kendra" button or fill out the application form. The free consult is the first real conversation — no pressure, no upsell.

Ready to stop quitting early?

One message. One free consult. No pressure. If it's not a fit, I'll tell you.
