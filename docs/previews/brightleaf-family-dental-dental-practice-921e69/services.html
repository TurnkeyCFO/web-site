General & Preventive Dentistry

The everyday care that keeps problems small.

Consistent preventive care is the single best thing you can do for your teeth — and it's the heart of what we do at Brightleaf.

Regular exams and professional cleanings catch decay, gum disease, and wear while they're still easy and affordable to treat. Our hygienists are gentle and thorough, and digital X-rays let us see clearly with minimal exposure. When a filling or repair is needed, we use tooth-colored materials and explain exactly why before we begin.

What's included

  • Comprehensive dental exams
  • Professional cleanings & polishing
  • Low-radiation digital X-rays
  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Gum-health evaluations
  • Fluoride treatment & sealants

Family & Children's Dentistry

A first dental visit that's actually fun.

How a child feels at the dentist early on shapes how they'll feel about it for life. We take that seriously.

Our team is patient, upbeat, and never rushed with younger patients. We explain tools in kid-friendly language, keep visits positive, and build the good habits that prevent problems later. And because Brightleaf has two dentists, parents and kids can often be seen in the same trip — one appointment, one parking spot, everyone done.

What's included

  • Gentle children's cleanings & exams
  • Sealants to protect young molars
  • Cavity prevention & coaching
  • Calm, kid-friendly explanations
  • Coordinated family scheduling
  • Care for every age, toddler to grandparent

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighter teeth, without the metal.

Invisalign uses a series of custom, nearly invisible aligners to gently move your teeth into place — no brackets, no wires.

It's a popular choice for working professionals and teens alike: the aligners are removable for meals and brushing, and most people won't even notice you're wearing them. We start with a digital scan and a clear plan so you can see your projected result before you commit — and we'll tell you honestly if traditional options would serve you better.

What's included

  • Digital smile scan & consultation
  • A custom, step-by-step treatment plan
  • Series of clear, removable aligners
  • Progress checks at a comfortable pace
  • Guidance for teens and adults
  • Retainer planning to protect results

Dental Implants

A permanent fix that feels like your own.

A dental implant replaces a missing tooth from the root up — giving you a result that looks natural and lasts.

Unlike a bridge or partial denture, an implant stands on its own without affecting neighboring teeth, and it restores full chewing strength. We take the time to assess whether an implant is right for you, walk you through each stage, and keep the whole process under one roof so you're never bouncing between offices. Everything is explained and priced before treatment begins.

What's included

  • Implant consultation & imaging
  • Single-tooth replacement
  • Implant-supported crowns
  • Natural-looking, durable materials
  • Clear, staged treatment timeline
  • Honest pricing before you decide

Teeth Whitening & Cosmetic

A brighter smile, done safely.

Professional whitening delivers results that drugstore strips simply can't — evenly, and without the sensitivity that comes from guessing.

We offer both in-office whitening for a noticeable change in a single visit and custom take-home trays for a gradual lift on your own schedule. For patients who want more, our cosmetic care also includes natural-looking bonding and veneers. The goal is never an overdone, artificial look — just your smile, brighter and more even.

What's included

  • In-office professional whitening
  • Custom take-home whitening trays
  • Tooth-colored cosmetic bonding
  • Porcelain veneers for makeovers
  • Shade matching for a natural result
  • Sensitivity-conscious technique

Emergency Dental Care

In pain today? We held a slot for you.

A dental emergency shouldn't mean a week on a waiting list. Brightleaf keeps same-day appointments open every single day.

Whether it's a sudden toothache, a chipped or knocked-out tooth, a lost filling or crown, or swelling that worries you — call us as early in the day as you can. Our front desk will triage your situation right away and get you seen, comfortable, and out of pain. If you're an existing patient or brand new to us, the answer is the same: we'll make room.

  • Severe toothaches & dental pain
  • Chipped, cracked, or broken teeth
  • Knocked-out teeth
  • Lost fillings or crowns
  • Swelling, abscesses & infections
  • Injuries from sports or accidents
