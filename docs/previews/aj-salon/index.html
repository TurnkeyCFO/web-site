Welcome
The Menu

Full-service hair, honest pricing.

Color, cut, and styling for the whole family. Tell us what you want — Amy and the team will tell you straight what works for your hair.

Women's Cuts & Styling

Precision shaping for short, mid-length, and the long-hair specialty Amy's known for. Includes shampoo, conditioning, and a finished blowout.

Color & Highlights

All-over color, balayage, foils, gloss, gray blending, and color correction. We use the consultation to set realistic, beautiful results before we ever pick up a brush.

Men's Cuts

Clipper work, scissor cuts, fades, and beard trims. Honest pricing, no-fuss appointments. Neighbors say we're the chain-salon alternative they've been looking for.

Kids' Cuts

Patient, friendly cuts for first-timers and everyday trims. The chair gets lower; the conversation gets sillier. We make it easy on parents.

Smoothing & Relaxers

Professional relaxers and smoothing treatments. We assess hair condition first and recommend the gentlest service that gets you the result you want.

Special-Occasion Styling

Updos, blowouts, and event-ready styling for weddings, graduations, dates, and family photos. Book in advance so we can plan the look with you.

Pricing varies by hair length, density, and service. Call for a fast quote — (817) 616-3110.
