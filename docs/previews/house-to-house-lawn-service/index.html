The round

Five stops, and your yard is handled.

Everything below is what the truck already carries. Book one, book all five — most folks start with a mow and end up on a standing route.

STOP 01

Mowing & edging

Clean cut, crisp edges along the walk and drive, and the clippings blown off your concrete before the trailer gate closes. Weekly or bi-weekly.

STOP 02

Hedges & shrubs

Red tips, boxwoods, foundation beds — shaped square and even, both sides of the house, then raked and bagged so the bed looks finished.

STOP 03

Tree care

Limbing up low branches, clearing the roofline and the driveway, and taking down what needs to come out. Ask Greg before storm season.

STOP 04

Fertilize & weed control

Feeding the turf and treating the weeds so the yard fills in thick instead of you fighting the same patches every spring.

STOP 05

Junk pickup & haul-off

Brush piles, old furniture, the stuff stacked behind the garage. Loaded up and gone in the same visit — no trip to the dump on your Saturday.

Not sure what your yard needs? Call (254) 661-1455 and Greg will tell you straight.

The owner

Greg Little answers the phone, and Greg shows up.

Greg came to Texas from Mississippi in the early nineties with the military and never left. House to House started the way most good route businesses do — one yard, done well, then the neighbor asks who did it.

That is still how it runs. Small crew, owner on the job, and customers who keep the same slot week after week. When you call, you get the man whose name is on the truck.
