Hey there, neighbor

A cozy spot to slow down in the heart of Rockport.

Bella la Brew has been nestled in the heart of the Rockport, TX area since 2014. This cozy little establishment is built on one simple passion — giving you a warm spot to relax and enjoy the company of others over a great cup of joe or a tasty treat.

We pour locally roasted, single-origin coffee, whip up frappes you can't get anywhere else, and stock the cases with fresh light eats and sweet treats. Then we wrapped a unique boutique around it all — so you can sip and shop in one stop.
