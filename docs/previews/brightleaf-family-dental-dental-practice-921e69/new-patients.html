Welcome to Brightleaf

Your first visit, made genuinely simple.

Switching dentists or finding one for the first time in Franklin shouldn't be a chore. Here's exactly what to expect — and everything you'll need to get started.

What to expect

Five easy steps from hello to healthy.

We've removed the friction that makes a new-patient visit stressful — the long forms, the insurance guesswork, the rushed exam. Here's how your first appointment really goes.

Plan for about an hour

A new-patient exam, cleaning, and X-rays are unhurried — and you'll always leave on time.

1

Request your appointment

Fill out the form below or call us. Tell us who the visit is for and whether anything is urgent — our front desk triages every request the same day.

2

We confirm & verify your benefits

A patient coordinator confirms a time that fits your schedule and checks your dental insurance in advance, so you know your coverage before you arrive.

3

A warm welcome & quick paperwork

Arrive a few minutes early to finish brief new-patient forms. We'll have your records request ready if you're transferring from another office.

4

A gentle, thorough exam

Your dentist and hygienist take their time — a careful cleaning, digital X-rays, an oral-health and gum check, and a look at anything on your mind.

5

A clear plan you control

We walk through what we found in plain language, with honest pricing. You decide what happens next — there's never any pressure.

Come prepared

What to bring to your first visit.

Have these handy and your check-in takes just a couple of minutes.

  • A photo ID
  • Your dental insurance card
  • A list of any medications you take
  • Recent X-rays, if you have them
  • Your previous dentist's name & contact
  • Any questions or concerns on your mind

Don't have old records or X-rays? No problem — just give us your previous dentist's details and we'll request everything for you.

Brightleaf Smile Membership

No insurance? No problem at all.

Plenty of our patients don't have dental insurance — and they still get consistent, affordable care through our in-house membership plan.

The Brightleaf Smile Membership covers your routine preventive care for one simple yearly fee, and includes a member discount on other treatments. There's no deductible, no annual maximum, and no claim forms — just predictable care that keeps your whole family on track.

Ask about membership

What membership includes

  • Routine cleanings & exams each year
  • Digital X-rays at no extra cost
  • A member discount on other treatments
  • No deductibles or annual maximums
  • No insurance claims or paperwork
  • Family-friendly enrollment

Membership pricing is set per practice — ask our team for current Brightleaf Smile Membership rates when you request a visit.
