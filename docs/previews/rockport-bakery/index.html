Our corner of the coast

Aqua walls, pink letters, and a fish tank in the back.

You'll know us by the bright aqua building on Business 35 with our name painted in pink. Inside it's pure Rockport — seashell tablecloths, floral displays, a fish tank, and a couple of parakeets keeping an eye on the donut case.

It's cluttered in the best way, it smells like heaven when the door swings open, and the folks behind the counter will tell you what's fresh and what the favorites are.
