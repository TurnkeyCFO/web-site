Grocery List for Fat Loss

The exact foods Kendra restocks every week. Protein-forward, real food, zero shake powder marketing. Print it. Bring it. Shop the perimeter first.

The rule: If 80% of what's in your cart is on this list, you'll lose fat without trying to lose fat. The list is the system.

Protein (pick 4-5)

  • Chicken breast / thighs
  • Lean ground turkey (93/7)
  • Lean ground beef (90/10)
  • Wild salmon (frozen is fine)
  • Shrimp (frozen)
  • Eggs (1-2 dozen)
  • Greek yogurt (2%, plain)
  • Cottage cheese (low-fat)
  • Canned tuna (in water)
  • Firm tofu
  • Edamame (frozen)

Carbs (pick 3-4)

  • Sweet potato
  • Yukon gold potato
  • Jasmine or basmati rice
  • Quinoa
  • Sprouted-grain bread (Ezekiel)
  • Steel-cut oats
  • Berries (frozen + fresh)
  • Bananas
  • Apples
  • Black beans / lentils (canned)

Vegetables (pick 5-6)

  • Spinach (big bag)
  • Mixed greens
  • Broccoli
  • Bell peppers
  • Cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Zucchini
  • Asparagus
  • Brussels sprouts
  • Onions + garlic
  • Carrots

Fats & flavor

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Avocados (2-3)
  • Raw almonds
  • Natural almond butter
  • Feta or goat cheese (small block)
  • Lemons
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro)
  • Sea salt, black pepper
  • Hot sauce, mustard, salsa
  • Balsamic vinegar

What's NOT on the list (and why)

  • Granola bars + protein bars. Candy in a wrapper for 95% of brands. Read labels.
  • Diet soda + zero-sugar drinks. Train your palate for plain water + sparkling water.
  • Crackers, chips, cereal. Engineered to overeat. Not on the list.
  • "Healthy" cookies, muffins, bakery items. Calorie-dense, low-protein, low-satisfaction.
