Meet the Grower

A Love of Wildflowers,
Rooted Here

Texas Sky Florals is built on open land, big blue skies, and the wildflowers that thrive in the summer sun. I was born and raised here — surrounded by bluebonnets and native blooms that cover the landscape each season — and that shaped how I see natural beauty and creation.

Over time, I began growing flowers myself. It started in backyard gardens, then a mobile flower cart, and now a working flower field. Today I focus on growing and sharing seasonal flowers through a roadside farmstand, "come and cut" field offerings, and hands-on workshops.

Alongside this, I design florals for select weddings and events — always guided by season and story. At the core, I don't see creation as random. I see it as intentional, and that perspective shapes everything I grow and design.

Aubri WestGrower & Floral Designer · Founder

How It Grew

From a Backyard
to a Flower Field

First

Backyard Gardens

It started with a few rows at home — learning the blooms, the soil, and the rhythm of a Texas season.

Then

A Mobile Cart

A flower cart took the blooms to the people — paper-wrapped bouquets, cut and shared close to home.

Now

A Working Field

Today it's rows of zinnias, cosmos and sunflowers — a farmstand, you-pick days, workshops, and wedding florals.
