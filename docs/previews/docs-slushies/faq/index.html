Doc's  /  FAQ

Good questions,
straight answers.

Everything you need to know about renting a frozen-drink machine from Doc's.

Do you provide or serve alcohol?+

No. Doc's rents the machine and provides the non-alcoholic slush mix. If you want spiked slushies, you supply your own alcohol and add it yourself. We never provide, pour, or serve alcohol — it's always BYO and your responsibility.

So what exactly am I renting?+

The frozen-drink machine (or machines), the slush mix, and cups — plus delivery, setup, and pickup. You choose your flavors; we get everything running and come back to break it down.

What areas do you serve?+

We're based in College Station and serve Bryan–College Station and the surrounding Brazos Valley. Traveling a bit farther for the right event? Just ask.

How much power and space do you need?+

Our machines fit a standard tailgate lot, backyard, or venue corner and run on a normal outlet. We'll confirm power and space when we scope your event, and plan around spots without easy power.

How many people does one machine serve?+

It depends on headcount and how long you want it pouring. We size the number of machines and flavors to your guest count so the line keeps moving the whole time.

How far ahead should I book?+

The sooner the better — game-day weekends and wedding season fill up fast in Aggieland. Send your date and we'll confirm availability and hold it for you.

Do you handle setup and cleanup?+

Always. We deliver, set up, and return for pickup and cleanup. You never touch the machines after the last pour.
