How it works

We rent the machines.
You run the party.

Doc's drops off the frozen-drink machines and the slush mix, gets everything pouring, and comes back for cleanup. Want spiked slushies? You supply the spirits and add them yourself — the machine and mix are on us.

Delivered & set up

We bring the machine to your spot in Bryan or College Station and walk you through how to use it. Live a bit further out? No sweat, we can probably make it happen for a reasonable travel fee.

Slush mix included

Non-alcoholic flavors on rotation — lemonade, cherry limeade, and more. All ages welcome.

You supply the spirits

Want it spiked? Bring your own alcohol and add it yourself. Doc's does not supply or serve alcohol. Always remember to drink (& host) responsibly!

FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

  • We currently serve Bryan, College Station, and the immediate surrounding areas.
  • A travel/trip fee may apply for events more than 20 miles outside our College Station home office.
  • All major credit cards, cash, Venmo, and Zelle.
  • All sales are subject to state and county sales tax (8.25%).
  • A non-refundable $100 deposit books and holds your event on our calendar.
  • Renting a 5000w generator from us requires an additional $500 refundable deposit, returned after we inspect the generator following your event.
  • Each machine has two ~15-liter (about 4-gallon) bowls.
  • We recommend one flavor per bowl — so two flavors per machine.
  • Our mixes are organic and made with cane sugar for a premium experience.
  • No. Your rental includes the slushie machine, the non-alcoholic mix, and any agreed-upon extras.
  • Every flavor comes with a mixing guide — sent before your event — showing exactly how much alcohol to add if you'd like spiked slushies. Always drink and host responsibly.
  • About 110 eight-ounce drinks per machine before a reload.
  • Each machine has two bowls holding up to 3.5 gallons.
  • We'll need access to your space at least 2 hours before start time so the machines reach proper temperature.
  • Our team delivers everything — machine, black rolling cart, cart skirt, mixes, bottled water, and any extras — to your address.
  • Once it's set up, we walk you (or an appointee) through how to run it.
  • You'll get a troubleshooting guide, and you're always welcome to call us during your event.
  • In an air-conditioned space (68–76°): about 60–90 minutes.
  • Outdoors or above 85°: about 2–3 hours.
  • Non-alcoholic mixes slush faster than spiked ones.
  • Minor temperature tweaks may be needed for the perfect consistency (non-alcoholic mixes slush around -2 to -5°C; spiked around -7 to -10°C — the higher the alcohol, the lower the temp).
  • Refill once you reach the "minimum level" line on the bowl. It takes about 30 minutes to get back to optimal slush after a refill.
  • Just turn off the machine when the party's over.
  • We pick up all our supplies at the agreed time — any leftover slush is yours to keep.
  • A quick water rinse is appreciated, but please don't take the machine apart. We deep-clean every machine after each event.
  • Our machines run on a standard 110-volt circuit and should be on their own dedicated outlet.
  • Avoid sharing the outlet with other appliances (microwaves, refrigerators, etc.) to prevent power interruptions or damage.
  • We recommend a 4000–5000 watt generator.
  • Initial cooling briefly surges to 2000–3000w, which trips underpowered generators — so leave headroom.
  • Once it's holding temperature, the machine only draws about 500–1000w, leaving plenty for swamp coolers, TVs, and more.
