About Kendra

Hi, I'm Kendra Washington.

Certified personal trainer, online fitness coach, and the reason 47 women across the country are getting stronger this month. I started EmpowerHer because the women I knew kept getting handed cookie-cutter programs that ignored their real lives.

Message Kendra → See programs

My approach: real coaching for real women.

I've been in this industry long enough to watch hundreds of women quit programs that were never built for them in the first place. Generic 12-week PDFs. Workouts designed for 22-year-old men. Nutrition plans that ignore stress, sleep, hormones, and life. The result: women blame themselves when the system was rigged from the start.

EmpowerHer is the opposite. Every plan starts with a conversation, not a template. We talk about your schedule, your training history, your recovery, the kind of food you actually want to eat, and the goals that actually matter to you. Then I build the plan around that — and adjust it every week as we go.

What I believe

Strength is the highest-leverage habit a woman can build. Not cardio, not crash diets, not chasing the scale — strength. Strong women sleep better, recover better, age better, and feel better in their own skin. Everything I coach is downstream of that.

Credentials

Certified Personal Trainer, women's health coaching specialty, years of in-person and virtual coaching experience. But what matters more than the credentials is the result: women who finish my programs, hit their goals, and stay strong on their own.
