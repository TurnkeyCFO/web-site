The idea
01

Dr. Hirsch tried the program himself. It worked. So he brought it into his practice.

Most weight loss programs hand you a box of food and a login. This one starts with a physician who has practiced family medicine in Broward County for more than three decades, and who watched the program work before he ever offered it to a patient.

Since 2017 he has offered it to his own patients and to anyone else in Pembroke Pines carrying weight they want off. You eat real food you buy yourself. You sit down with someone one-on-one. And your health is watched the whole way through.

Stage One

You sit down with the doctor.

The first visit is a real medical consult, not an intake form. Dr. Hirsch reviews your history, what you have already tried, and what has gotten in the way, then tells you honestly whether this program is the right fit for you. If it is not, he will say so.

Stage Two

A plan built around your life.

Your plan is personalized, and it uses real food you buy at your own grocery store. There are no prepackaged meals to order and no gym membership to keep up with. The program is designed to work without an exercise requirement, so a demanding job or a bad knee does not end it in week two.

Stage Three

Someone in your corner, on a schedule.

One-on-one counseling is the part patients talk about most. You are checked on, encouraged, and adjusted. Because this sits inside a family practice, supporting options like B12 shots and lipotropic injections are available on site when they are appropriate for you.

Stage Four

Long term, not just this month.

The point of doing this with a physician is what comes after the weight comes off. Dr. Hirsch has seen meaningful improvement in patients' overall health after the program, along with weight loss that holds. And he is still your doctor when the program ends.

On offer

What the practice actually offers.

The weight loss program is the front door. Behind it is a full family practice, which is why your bloodwork, your allergies, and your primary care can all live under one roof.
