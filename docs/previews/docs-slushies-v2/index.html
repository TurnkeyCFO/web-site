FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

Do you provide or serve alcohol?

No. Doc's rents the machine and provides the non-alcoholic slush mix. If you want spiked slushies, you supply your own alcohol and add it yourself. We never provide, pour, or serve alcohol — it's always BYO and your responsibility.

What exactly am I renting?

The machine (or machines), the slush mix, and cups — plus delivery, setup, and pickup. You choose your flavors; we get everything running and come back to break it down.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in College Station and serve Bryan–College Station and the surrounding Brazos Valley. Traveling a bit farther for the right event? Just ask.

How far ahead should I book?

The sooner the better — game-day weekends and wedding season fill up fast in Aggieland. Send your date and we'll hold it for you.
