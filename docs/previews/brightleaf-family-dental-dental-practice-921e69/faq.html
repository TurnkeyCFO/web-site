Questions & answers

The things patients most want to know.

From insurance and emergencies to kids' visits and office hours — here's everything new patients ask before they call. Still not sure? We're glad to help.

New Patients & Appointments

Is Brightleaf Family Dental accepting new patients?

Yes. Brightleaf Family Dental is actively welcoming new patients of all ages from Franklin, Brentwood, and the surrounding Williamson County area. You can request a visit through our appointment form or by calling (615) 770-3344.

How do I request an appointment?

The fastest way is the appointment form on our New Patients or Contact page. Tell us who the visit is for, your reason for visiting, and whether it is urgent. You can also call us at (615) 770-3344 and our front desk will help you directly.

How soon can I be seen as a new patient?

We aim to schedule new-patient visits promptly and offer evening and early appointment times. After you send a request, a patient coordinator typically follows up the same business day to confirm a time that fits your schedule. If you have a dental emergency, we hold same-day slots open every day.

Can my whole family be seen on the same day?

Often, yes. Because Brightleaf has two dentists on staff, we can coordinate back-to-back family appointments so parents and children can be seen in a single trip. Let us know when you request your visit and we will do our best to schedule everyone together.

Insurance & Payment

What dental insurance do you accept?

We accept most major dental plans, including Delta Dental, Cigna, MetLife, Aetna, Guardian, BlueCross BlueShield, United Concordia, and Humana, along with most PPO plans. We file your claims for you and verify your specific benefits before your visit.

What if I do not have dental insurance?

Uninsured patients are very welcome. The Brightleaf Smile Membership covers your routine preventive care for one simple yearly fee, with a member discount on other treatments and no deductibles, annual maximums, or claim forms. Ask our team for current membership pricing when you request a visit.

Will I know the cost of treatment before it begins?

Always. We explain every recommended treatment in plain language and provide honest pricing before anything begins. You will never receive a surprise bill, and you are never pressured into care you did not ask about.

Dental Emergencies

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call us as early in the day as you can at (615) 770-3344. We hold same-day emergency appointments open every day, and our front desk will triage your situation right away to get you seen and out of pain. For severe swelling, trauma, or bleeding that will not stop, seek urgent medical care.

What counts as a dental emergency?

Common dental emergencies include a severe toothache, a chipped, cracked, broken, or knocked-out tooth, a lost filling or crown, and swelling or signs of infection. If you are unsure whether your situation is urgent, call us and we will help you decide.

Do I have to be an existing patient for emergency care?

No. We provide same-day emergency dental care to both existing patients and new patients. If you are in pain, call us and we will make room for you.

Treatments & Visits

What happens at a new-patient visit?

A new-patient visit usually takes about an hour. It includes brief paperwork, a thorough cleaning, low-radiation digital X-rays, and an unhurried exam of your teeth and gums. Afterward, your dentist walks through what they found in plain language and gives you a clear, prioritized plan with honest pricing.

Do you treat children?

Yes. Brightleaf is a family practice and we care for patients of every age, from a child's first cleaning through their teen years and beyond. Our team is patient and upbeat with younger patients and focuses on making early dental visits a positive experience.

Is Invisalign available at Brightleaf?

Yes. We offer Invisalign clear aligners for teens and adults. Treatment begins with a digital scan and a clear plan so you can understand your projected result before you commit. We will also tell you honestly if another option would serve you better.

I get anxious at the dentist — can you help?

Absolutely. Anxious patients are welcome at Brightleaf. We use gentle techniques, take time to explain each step, and you can pause at any point. Many patients tell us that a calm, no-pressure visit changed how they feel about going to the dentist.

What are your office hours?

Brightleaf Family Dental is open Monday through Thursday from 8:00am to 5:00pm and Friday from 8:00am to 2:00pm. Saturday visits are available by appointment. Same-day emergency slots are held open every day we are open.

Didn't find your answer?

Our front desk is happy to help with anything specific to you or your family. Call (615) 770-3344 or send a note through our contact form.
