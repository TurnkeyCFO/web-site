What We Offer

Two ways to bring the blooms.

A roaming flower bar for the in-between celebrations — and a full-service wedding studio for the day itself.

Mobile Flower Bar

We bring a curated bar of in-season stems, foliage, wraps, and shears to your event. Your guests pick, wrap, and walk out with a bouquet they built — a favor and an experience in one.

  • ✦ Bridal showers, bachelorettes, birthdays
  • ✦ Brand activations and corporate gifting
  • ✦ Baby showers, holiday markets, pop-ups

Bridal and bridesmaid bouquets, boutonnières, ceremony arches, centerpieces, hanging installs, and everything in between — designed to your palette and your venue.

Our Story

Built around the moment — not the bouquet alone.

Flower Street started as a single mobile bouquet bar at a friend's bridal shower in South Austin. The reaction was the same every time — guests lingering, picking stems they'd never thought to put together, leaving with something they'd actually keep. That's when we knew flowers were always supposed to be an experience.

We pulled the same instinct into our wedding work: garden-style, locally designed, in-season. Untamed shapes, soft palettes, and venues from East Austin lofts to Hill Country estates.
