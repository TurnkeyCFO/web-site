You're welcome here

A church family for every generation

Bethany is a welcoming Pentecostal church in Abbotsford, serving families across the Fraser Valley and the Lower Mainland. Week by week, we gather to worship, to pray, and to grow together in God's Word.

Our services are held in Romanian, and our heart is for the whole family — from the youngest children to the seasoned saints who built this church. Wherever you're starting from, you'll find a warm welcome and a place to belong.

Our Ministries

Ways to grow & get involved

There's a place for every age and every season of faith at Bethany.

Worship & Prayer

Heartfelt worship and prayer services where the whole church lifts its voice together — the heartbeat of our week.

Bible Study

Studying the Scriptures together so faith is built on a sure foundation — for new believers and lifelong followers alike.

Kids' Ministry

Fun, caring classes for children and teens — teaching the next generation to know and love Jesus.

Youth & Orchestra

A vibrant youth ministry and a youth orchestra — a place for young people to grow, serve, and use their gifts.

Community Outreach

Sharing the love of Christ through outreach to communities across the Fraser Valley and the Lower Mainland.

Baptisms & Dedications

Walking with families through the milestones of faith — baptism, weddings, and the dedication of children.
