The B & W in the name

A family business,
in the truest sense.

B&W is Bill and Wanda — a husband-and-wife team who started serving Livingston County back in 1989. More than three decades later, the same values still run the crew: show up, do it safely, leave the yard clean, and charge a fair price.

We're not the biggest outfit around, and we like it that way. When you call B&W, you get people who actually live and work in these towns — Pinckney, Gregory, Hell, Chelsea, and Dexter — and who plan on being here for the next thirty years, too.
