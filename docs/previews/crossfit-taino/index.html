WHO WE ARE

Working hard
should be fun.

We built CrossFit Taino to be a room you actually enjoy being a part of. When you have a good time, walking back through the doors tomorrow gets easy, and that is the whole game. We are here to help you set real goals and get stronger, physically and mentally.

The workouts are varied and genuinely challenging. Our WODs scale to whoever shows up, so the person on day one and the person on year six are in the same class, working just as hard.

Fitness

Lose body fat, hold onto lean muscle, feel better in your body. Eager to learn new movements, not interested in competing.

Performance

Be better at the things you love, from climbing to pickup basketball. Challenged hard every day, on your terms.

THE MARK

The name on the door
is not decoration.

The Taíno were the indigenous people of the Caribbean. Our mark is a thunderbird carved in that petroglyph line, wings open, carrying the CT shield at its chest. It is the first thing you see on the wall and the last thing you see on your way out.

It stands for the thing this gym is actually built on. You are not a membership number here. You show up, you get coached by name, and the room notices when you are gone.
