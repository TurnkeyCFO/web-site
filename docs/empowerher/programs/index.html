Which program is right for you?

If you're brand new to consistent training or coming back after a long break, the Self-Paced program is your launchpad — a structured 12-week program with enough hand-holding to keep you moving.

If you've trained on and off for years and you're ready to finally see real, measurable results, 1:1 Coaching is the highest-impact tier. Everything is built for you, adjusted weekly, and you have Kendra in your pocket by text. This is where the transformations happen.

If you want the accountability of a live session with eyes on your form in real time, Live Sessions give you that face-to-face element online clients often miss.
