Before you come in

Bring these and you are in and out.

A general checklist for a straightforward return. Tap each one as you put it in the folder. Missing something? Come anyway and we will tell you what is still needed.

Folder ready 0/6

Tap the list to check things off.

Photo ID Driver’s license or state ID for you and your spouse.
Social Security cards For everyone on the return, including each dependent.
Income forms W‑2s from every job, plus any 1099s for contract or side work.
Last year’s return If you filed somewhere else. It speeds everything up.
Bank account details Routing and account number, for direct deposit of a refund.
Anything that looks official Mortgage interest, tuition, childcare, IRS letters. If in doubt, put it in the folder.
