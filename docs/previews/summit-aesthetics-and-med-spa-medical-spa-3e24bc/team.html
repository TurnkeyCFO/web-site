Our People

The team behind a result that always looks like you.

Aesthetic medicine is only as good as the hands performing it. Meet the leadership and the standard every Summit provider is held to.

Leadership

The two people accountable for every Summit standard.

Alexandra Whitmore

Founder & Managing Partner

Alexandra founded Summit to close the gap she kept running into as a client — spas that were either clinical or beautiful, but rarely consistent. She leads the company’s standards, culture and the four-location experience, and is still the first to ask whether a recommendation is genuinely in the client’s interest.

Dr. Chen

Medical Director

Dr. Chen provides medical oversight across all four Summit locations. Every treatment protocol, candidacy decision and clinical claim is reviewed under this supervision. It is the reason “medical” in our name is a promise, not a label — and the reason our injectors practice the way they do.

The Standard Every Provider Meets

We do not name every team member here — we tell you what each one is required to be.

Across all four locations, every provider is hired, trained and re-trained to the same Summit protocols. Consistency is not an accident; it is a standard.

Master-Certified Injectors

Our injectors hold advanced, master-level injectable certification and train to an anatomy-first, conservative standard. They are the reason a Summit result looks subtle — and even across every location.

Licensed Medical Aestheticians

Our facials, peels and microneedling are performed by licensed medical aestheticians who understand skin clinically — so a ‘facial’ here is a corrective treatment, not just an hour of relaxation.

Certified Laser Specialists

Energy-based devices demand real expertise, especially on diverse skin tones in Arizona’s sun. Our laser specialists are certified and device-specific trained for safe, calibrated results.

Patient Care Coordinators

From your first question to your follow-up, a care coordinator keeps your plan, your credits and your communication on track — the one point of contact our clients tell us they missed elsewhere.

How We Hire

Talent first — then trained to the Summit way.

Certifications get a provider in the room. What keeps them on the Summit team is judgment: the discipline to recommend less, the eye for a natural result, and the warmth to make a clinical visit feel like a calm one.

New providers shadow, train and are signed off on Summit protocols before they treat independently — and continue education throughout the year. It is unglamorous, and it is exactly why your result does not depend on which location or which day you booked.
