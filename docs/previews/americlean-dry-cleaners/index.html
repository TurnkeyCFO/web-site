The cleaning

Everyday shirts, work suits, dresses and formalwear. Stains get looked at individually before anything goes through, and everything comes back pressed properly rather than just heat-flattened.

  • Hemming and length adjustments

How it works

What happens between drop-off and done

Swipe to follow the steps

01

Drop it off

Walk in at 8960 Taft Street. Tell us what it is and what you need. You get a ticket with your pickup time on it.

02

We inspect it

Every piece is checked for stains, fabric type and anything loose before it goes anywhere near a machine.

03

Clean or tailor

Cleaning goes one way, alterations go to the seamstress. If you asked for both, it does both.

04

Pressed and finished

Steamed, pressed and hung. This is the step that decides whether it looks cared for or just washed.

05

Ready when we said

Come back at the time on your ticket. Not sure it’s ready? Call ahead and we’ll check for you.
