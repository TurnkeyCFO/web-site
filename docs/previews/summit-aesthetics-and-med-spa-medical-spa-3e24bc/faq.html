Treatments & Results

03  ·  Membership & Pricing

Membership & Pricing

It is our membership program. For a monthly fee starting at $99, you earn a treatment credit that rolls over for up to six months, unlock member pricing on injectables, laser and facials, receive complimentary facials and a retail discount, and get priority booking. If you treat more than twice a year, it usually pays for itself.

Always. Your Summit Society membership, credit balance and full client record work identically at all four locations. Book Scottsdale this month and Chandler the next — nothing changes.

Aesthetic treatment is personal, so your exact investment is confirmed in writing at your complimentary consultation — never quoted under pressure. Our published prices are genuine starting points. Financing is available on larger plans through Cherry, and pre-paid packages lock in series pricing.

We ask for 24 hours’ notice to reschedule a treatment appointment so we can offer the time to another client. Memberships are month-to-month after a three-month minimum and can be paused or cancelled with 30 days’ notice.
