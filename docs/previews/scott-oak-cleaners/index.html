What we care for

If you can wear it or sleep under it, bring it in.

Everything is inspected before it is cleaned and checked again before it goes on the rail. If something needs a second pass, it gets one.

Ask about your item
01

Dry Cleaning

Suits, jackets, dresses, blouses, slacks, and coats. Cleaned, pressed, and returned on the hanger, often the next day.

02

Shirt Laundry

Laundered and pressed dress shirts, done by the piece or by the stack. Collars and cuffs get the attention they actually need.

03

In-House Alterations

Hems, tapers, waistbands, sleeves, and zippers, handled here rather than sent out. Bring the shoes you plan to wear.

05

The process

What happens between drop-off and hanger.

01

Inspect & tag

Every piece is looked over and tagged by hand. Stains get noted before anything touches a machine, so nothing gets missed later.

02

Treat the trouble

Spots are worked individually first. If a stain is unlikely to come out, you hear that up front instead of at pickup.

03

Clean & press

Cleaned to the fabric, then finished and pressed. Delicate pieces and gowns are handled by hand rather than rushed through.

04

Checked, then ready

A second look before it goes on the rail. Anything that is not right goes back. Often ready the next day.
