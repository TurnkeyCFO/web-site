What We Do

Color, blowouts, and everything in between.

Tell us the look you're after and we'll tell you straight what works for your hair. Honest advice, careful hands, and a finish you'll love.

Hair Color

All-over color, root touch-ups, gloss, gray blending, and color correction. We start with a real consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

Balayage & Highlights

Hand-painted balayage, ombre, foilyage, and classic foils for soft, sun-kissed dimension. Lived-in color that grows out beautifully.

Blowouts & Styling

Smooth, voluminous blowouts and finished styling for any occasion — or just because. Walk out feeling put-together and polished.

Treatments & Smoothing

Keratin smoothing, perms, and conditioning treatments. We assess your hair first and recommend the gentlest option to reach your goal.

Cuts for the Family

Women's cuts, men's cuts, fades, and patient kids' trims. Clean shaping and honest pricing for everyone who walks through the door.

Updos & Special Events

Updos, extensions, and event-ready looks for weddings, quinceañeras, graduations, and photos. Book ahead so we can plan your day.

Pricing varies by hair length, density, and service. Call for a quick quote — (214) 241-3233.
