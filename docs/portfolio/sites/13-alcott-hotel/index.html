Our Story

A hotel built on the art of staying.

The Alcott opened in a 1920s South Congress building that had served as a printing press, a speakeasy, and at one point, a dance school. We kept the bones and built the rest around them.

Every piece of furniture was sourced or commissioned from Austin makers. Every piece of art was acquired directly from the artists. There is no brand standard here — only taste.
