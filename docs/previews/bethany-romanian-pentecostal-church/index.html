Our Church

Rooted in the Word, gathered as family.

Bethany is a Romanian-speaking Pentecostal congregation in Abbotsford, in the heart of British Columbia's Fraser Valley. Week by week, families come together to worship, pray, and open the Scriptures — in the language that feels most like home.

From children's classes and a youth orchestra to outreach across the Lower Mainland, the life of our church reaches every generation. We believe the Gospel is good news for the whole family — and the door is always open to anyone who wants to come and see.

Life of the Church

Ways we gather & serve.

There's a place for every age and every season of faith at Bethany — from the youngest children to the seasoned saints who built this church.

Worship & Prayer

Spirit-filled worship and prayer services in the Romanian language — the heart of our week, where the whole church lifts its voice together.

Bible Study & Teaching

Studying the Scriptures together so faith is built on a sure foundation — for new believers and lifelong followers of Christ alike.

Children's Ministry

Instructional classes for children and teenagers — teaching the next generation to know and love Jesus in word and song.

Youth & Orchestra

A vibrant youth ministry and a youth orchestra that practices and performs — giving young people a place to grow, serve, and use their gifts.

Community Outreach

Sharing the love of Christ through outreach to communities across the Fraser Valley and the Lower Mainland.

Baptisms & Dedications

Walking with families through the milestones of faith — believer's baptism, weddings, and the dedication of children to the Lord.
