The flooring reality

Why a real website wins for flooring companies specifically.

Flooring is a considered, visual, high-ticket purchase. The homeowner's decision-making process is fundamentally different from an emergency service call — and your website has to match that journey.

Flooring is bought with the eyes

Seeing is believing — and booking

No homeowner chooses flooring without seeing examples. A gallery of your actual installs — in real Texas homes, in the style ranges you offer — does more selling than any written description. It lets them see their own floors before you ever visit.

The research phase happens online

They've narrowed it to three before they call

Homeowners typically research multiple flooring companies before requesting an estimate. The companies with clear websites, strong galleries, and helpful material guides make the shortlist. Those without one rarely do.

It's a big-ticket, trust-required purchase

Credibility converts at this price point

A full-room flooring install is a significant investment for most families. A professional website — with reviews, photos, and clear service information — signals that you're an established company they can trust with their home, not someone who might disappear mid-job.

After-hours browsing drives weekday calls

The decision happens at 9pm on the couch

Most flooring research happens in the evening when homeowners are relaxed and scrolling. A website with a working estimate request form captures that moment — so Monday morning starts with a stack of warm leads from the weekend's browsing sessions.

Flooring FAQ

Questions flooring companies ask us first.

Homeowners already find me through referrals — do I really need a website?

Referrals are great, but the first thing every referral does is search your name online to check you out before they call. With no site or a weak one, that warm lead hesitates. A professional website validates your quality, shows your gallery, and captures the homeowners who are searching "flooring company near me" on their own — leads that word of mouth never reaches.

What if I already have a Facebook page or Houzz profile?

Those platforms help, but you don't own them. Algorithm changes, platform fees, or account issues can make your presence disappear overnight. Your own website is an asset you control entirely — and it's the page Google shows first when someone searches for a flooring company in your city.

How will a website actually help me get more flooring estimate requests?

A well-built flooring website does three things: it ranks for local searches like "hardwood flooring installation Austin", it shows your past work in a gallery that makes homeowners picture their own floors, and it gives them an easy way to request a free in-home estimate any time — including evenings and weekends when you're not available to answer the phone.

Will my flooring website rank on Google?

Every site we build ships SEO-ready: proper title tags, local-business schema, service-area pages, and fast mobile load — the technical foundations Google rewards. Combined with your Google Business Profile, you'll have a real shot at showing up when homeowners search for flooring companies in your service area.

How quickly can my flooring website be live?

Your first complete draft is delivered within 7 days of your brand questionnaire. Most flooring sites are fully live within 2 to 3 weeks, including your photo gallery and estimate request form.

What does the $250 setup fee actually include?

Everything required to launch: design, build, copy, photo gallery setup, mobile optimization, on-page SEO, hosting, SSL certificate, and domain connection — plus unlimited revisions until you're thrilled with the result. The same $250 covers a one-page site or a ten-page site. Then $50/mo covers hosting, management, and unlimited future updates.

Do I need to provide all my photos and write all the content?

You share what you have — phone photos of past jobs are completely fine — and we handle the rest: layout, copywriting, optimization, and design. If your photo library is thin, we can supplement with high-quality stock imagery until your project gallery fills in.
