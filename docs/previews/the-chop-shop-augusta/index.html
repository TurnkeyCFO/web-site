The Barber

Trained old-school.
Cutting like it’s an art.

Daniel earned his Master Barber license in 2004 out of Southern Barber College and has spent the last two decades in the same downtown chair, quietly building a reputation as one of the best in the city. He cuts every texture, sees the shape before the clippers move, and never rushes a line.

Regulars keep coming back for a simple reason: you leave looking your best, every time. First-timers usually become regulars.
