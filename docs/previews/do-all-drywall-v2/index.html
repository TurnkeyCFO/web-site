How it's done

Four passes to a
finish you live with.

Anyone can hang board. The finish is what you see every day — so we work it in four deliberate passes, and we don't call it done until the light proves it's smooth.
01

Hang

Board cut tight and screwed off square — flat planes and clean corners set up everything that follows.

02

Tape & Mud

Seams taped and bedded, fasteners filled — the first coats that turn separate sheets into one surface.

03

Sand & Texture

Sanded smooth, then textured to match the room — knockdown, orange-peel, or dead-flat for paint.

04

Light Check

We rake a light across the wall to catch anything the eye misses — so what you get is genuinely flat.
