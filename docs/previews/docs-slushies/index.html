How it works

We rent the machines.
You run the party.

Doc's drops off the frozen-drink machines and the slush mix, gets everything pouring, and comes back for cleanup. Want spiked slushies? You supply the spirits and add them yourself — the machine and mix are on us.

Delivered & set up

We bring the machines to your spot in College Station and the Brazos Valley and get them running.

Slush mix included

Non-alcoholic flavors on rotation — lemonade, cherry limeade, and more. All ages welcome.

You supply the spirits

Want it spiked? Bring your own alcohol and add it yourself. Doc's never supplies or serves alcohol.

We handle cleanup

Pickup and cleanup are on us. You never touch the machines after the last pour.
