House Cleaning FAQ

Questions cleaning businesses ask us first.

I get most of my cleaning clients from referrals. Do I really need a website?

Referrals still matter — but the person your client just referred you to will Google your name before they ever text you. With no site, that warm lead hesitates, then hires someone who looks more established. A real website confirms you're vetted, insured, and easy to book — and it captures new clients referrals never reach.

Why not just use an app like Handy or Thumbtack?

Those platforms rent you clients, take a cut of every job, and pit you against a dozen competitors on price. Your own website is an asset you own outright. It builds your brand, collects your own reviews, and costs a flat $50/mo — no commissions, no race to the bottom on price.

Can my website actually let clients book online?

Yes. We connect your site to a booking tool so new and returning clients can schedule a clean, choose their frequency, and pay a deposit — all without you picking up the phone. Recurring bookings especially benefit: clients lock in their slot automatically.

How do I show clients I'm trustworthy when they're letting me into their home?

Your website is where trust is built before the first call. We highlight your background-check status, bonding and insurance, real client reviews, and a professional-looking brand so a homeowner feels confident before they ever hand over a key.
