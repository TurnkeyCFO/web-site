Why a real detail

A drive-through wash and a Red Oak detail are not the same thing.

A tunnel wash rinses the surface. A proper detail protects your investment, restores the finish, and makes a vehicle feel new again — without you ever leaving home.

A detail with Red Oak
  • We arrive at your home or office — zero driving, zero waiting room
  • Hand wash and decontamination that protect the clear coat, not abrade it
  • Interior deep-cleaned: carpets, upholstery, leather, vents and every crevice
  • Real protection — wax, sealant or ceramic coating that lasts months
  • One detailer who knows your vehicle and stands behind the result
  • Booked online with a clear price you saw before you committed
The car-wash compromise
  • You drive there, wait in line, and lose part of your day
  • Spinning brushes that can leave swirl marks across your paint
  • Interior barely touched — a quick vacuum at best
  • No lasting protection; the finish is exposed again within days
  • A different crew every visit, rushing to the next car
  • Surprise upsells at the register instead of upfront pricing

Good questions

Everything you'd want to know first.

Do I have to be home during the detail?
Not necessarily. As long as the vehicle is accessible and there is a water source and outdoor outlet nearby, many customers leave a key and go about their day. We send a message when we arrive and when the vehicle is finished.
What do you need from me on the day of the appointment?
A spot to park the vehicle — a driveway, carport or office lot is ideal — plus access to a standard water spigot and a regular electrical outlet. If a water or power source isn't available, let us know when you book and we can plan around it.
How long does a detail take?
An Express Refresh usually takes about an hour. A Full Interior & Exterior Detail typically runs two to three hours, and a Showroom Detail or ceramic coating can take most of the day. We confirm a realistic time window when your appointment is booked.
What areas do you serve?
Red Oak Mobile Detailing serves Austin and the northern suburbs, including Round Rock, Cedar Park and Pflugerville, plus nearby communities such as Leander, Georgetown and Hutto. If you're just outside that area, reach out — we'll let you know if we can make it work.
Can you book me without texting back and forth?
Yes — that's the whole point of the online booking form. Choose your package, vehicle type and a preferred date, send your details, and we confirm your appointment by message. No phone tag required.
Do you detail trucks and SUVs?
Absolutely. Every package is priced by vehicle size — car, truck or SUV, and extra-large vehicles such as lifted trucks or three-row SUVs. The booking form lets you pick your vehicle type so your quote is accurate.
