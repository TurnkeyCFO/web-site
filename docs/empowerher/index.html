FAQ

Questions, answered.

How much does coaching cost?
Programs are tiered, with most clients investing the equivalent of a gym membership for a fully personalized plan, weekly check-ins, and direct access to a coach. Message Kendra for current rates and tier details — she will help you pick the right fit.
Is this just for advanced women?
No — about half of new clients are total beginners, including women who have not worked out consistently in years. Plans are built around your starting point, schedule, and equipment.
I have quit programs before — how is this different?
Weekly check-ins, an app for daily accountability, and direct text access to Kendra mean you do not slip through the cracks the way you do in a self-led program. The accountability piece is the whole reason this works.
How much time per week do I need?
Plans are typically 3 to 4 sessions per week, 30 to 45 minutes each. Kendra will design around your real schedule — busy parents, shift workers, and travelers are all here.
Do you do nutrition too?
Yes. Every program includes nutrition guidance — protein targets, easy swaps, and food-first habits. No crash diets, no shake plans, no off-limits foods.
Is EmpowerHer Fitness only for women?
Yes — the coaching, programming, and community are designed specifically for women.
How is this different from a gym membership?
A gym gives you equipment. EmpowerHer gives you a coach. Every workout is built for you, every week is reviewed, and you have direct access to Kendra by text.
What if I have an injury or limitation?
Tell Kendra everything on your intake call. Plans are built around your body — if something hurts, the plan changes. Several current clients are working around old injuries.
Do you offer a free intro call?
Yes — every new client starts with a free 20-minute discovery call. We talk goals, history, and whether we are a fit. No pressure, no upsell.
What time zone are live sessions in?
Kendra coaches across multiple US time zones. Tell her when you are free and she will work it into the schedule.

Online fitness coaching that meets you where you are.

If you have been searching for a virtual personal trainer for women, you already know the problem. Most online programs are PDFs in disguise — generic templates that ignore your schedule, your starting strength, your injury history, and the actual reasons you have quit before. EmpowerHer Fitness is built on the opposite premise: that real results come from a real coach who knows your name.

Why women-only coaching works

Women's bodies, hormones, and recovery patterns are not the same as men's, and a coaching framework that pretends otherwise leaves results on the table. Kendra's training and nutrition approach is calibrated for women in their 30s, 40s, and 50s — the demographic most underserved by mainstream fitness content.

What "online" really means here

Online coaching does not mean self-serve. You get a custom plan in the app, weekly check-ins where Kendra reviews your week and updates your program, direct text access for the inevitable "is this right?" moments, and form-check video reviews when you want eyes on a lift. It is closer to having a personal trainer than most in-person gym memberships.

Who EmpowerHer is for

Busy professionals, mothers, women returning to training after a long break, women who have never trained before, and women who want to get serious about strength after years of cardio and crash diets. See the program tiers or see what clients have done.
