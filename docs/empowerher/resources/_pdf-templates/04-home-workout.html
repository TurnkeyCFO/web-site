Home Workout — No Equipment Needed

Twelve bodyweight movements that build real strength. Three 25-minute circuits you can do in your living room, hotel room, or backyard. No gym, no excuses.

How it works: Each circuit is 5 rounds. Do all movements in order, rest 60 seconds between rounds. Stop a round short if form breaks. Form is everything.

The 12 Movements

MovementCue
Air SquatKnees track over toes, sit back into hips, chest up.
Reverse LungeStep back, drop knee toward floor, drive through front heel.
Glute BridgeHeels down, ribs down, squeeze glutes 1-second at top.
Single-Leg Glute BridgeOne leg straight, drive other heel through floor.
Push-Up (knees OK)Elbows at 45 degrees, body in straight line.
Incline Push-Up (hands on couch)Easier variation. Same form.
Bird-DogOpposite arm + leg, hold 2 seconds, no hip rocking.
Dead BugLower back glued to floor, slow control.
PlankStraight line, ribs down, breathe.
Side PlankHip lifted, body in straight line.
Step-Up (chair / stair)Drive through heel, don't bounce off back foot.
Wall SitThighs parallel to floor, breathe.

Circuit A — Lower Body Focus

5 rounds, 60 sec rest between rounds.
  • Air Squat · 15 reps
  • Reverse Lunge · 10 each leg
  • Glute Bridge · 15 reps
  • Wall Sit · 30 seconds

Circuit B — Upper Body + Core

5 rounds, 60 sec rest between rounds.
  • Push-Up · 8-12 reps
  • Bird-Dog · 8 each side
  • Plank · 30-45 seconds
  • Dead Bug · 8 each side

Circuit C — Full Body

5 rounds, 60 sec rest between rounds.
  • Air Squat · 12 reps
  • Push-Up · 8-10 reps
  • Step-Up · 10 each leg
  • Side Plank · 20 sec each side
