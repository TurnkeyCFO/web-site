What we do

The full canopy of care — from crown to stump.

Whether you've got a single palm leaning over the pool or a storm-damaged oak threatening the house, Mike's crew shows up on time, works clean, and leaves your yard looking better than when we arrived.

✦

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Shape, thin and lift your canopy for a healthier tree and a safer yard. We handle live oaks, pecans, palms — the whole Gulf Coast lineup.

✦

Full Tree Removal

Dead, dying, leaning, or just in the wrong place — we take it down piece by piece, with rigging when the space is tight and care every step.

✦

Stump Grinding

Don't live with that stump for one more season. We grind it below grade so you can sod, plant or just stop tripping over it.

✦

Palm Tree Service

Skinned, trimmed and shaped right — the way a palm should look. Especially the ones leaning over a pool, deck or driveway.

✦

Brush & Lot Clearing

Overgrown lot? Storm cleanup? Hauling? We clear it, chip it, haul it off — and leave the property looking ready to build, sell, or just sit in.

✦

Landscaping

Once the tree work is done, we'll help you put the yard back together — fresh sod, mulch, hedge trimming and clean-line landscape touch-ups.
