More than childcare — a true second home.

Founded in 2009 by a former elementary school principal, Bright Minds was built on a single premise: children thrive when they feel loved, challenged, and genuinely seen.

Our 8:1 child-to-teacher ratio is well below the state requirement. Because the right number isn't the minimum — it's whatever your child needs.

  • NAEYC nationally accredited since 2011
  • All teachers hold ECE degrees or certifications
  • Healthy, chef-prepared meals and snacks daily
  • Parent portal app with daily updates and photos
