What we fix

Everything that
bends, breaks
or stops lining up.

Cartwright is a full body shop, not a parts swapper. Collision work, frame straightening, refinishing and alignment all happen under the same roof on Loop 12 — which is why the car leaves fitting the way it did before.

Talk it through: (972) 579-8868

Collision & body repair

Rear-end damage, crumpled quarters, doors and fenders. Panels repaired or replaced so the gaps come back even.

Frame & unibody

Frame testing and straightening. If the structure moved in the hit, it gets measured and pulled back before anything cosmetic happens.

Paint & refinishing

Color matched and blended into the surrounding panels, then cleared. The repair should disappear, not announce itself.

Wheel alignment

On the sign since day one. Steering and suspension checked and set so it tracks straight and stops eating tires.

Bumper repair

Scuffs, transfer marks and cracked covers. Often repairable rather than replaceable, which is usually the cheaper honest answer.

Mechanical work

Brakes, air conditioning, electrical, radiators and general repair, including foreign makes. Ask and we will tell you straight if it is in our lane.

The refinish

Three stages,
no guessing.

01

Look and level

Damage assessed, frame measured, panels straightened and filled flat. You get the price and the plan at this point, not after.

02

Prime and prep

Sealed, blocked and masked. The dull grey stage nobody photographs is the one that decides whether the finish holds.

03

Color and clear

Matched, blended into the neighboring panels and cleared. Then alignment checked so it drives the way it looks.
