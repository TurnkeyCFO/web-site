What we self-perform

The groundwork before the first slab is poured.

Brady Construction of Ocala is a certified underground utility and excavation contractor. We take a raw parcel and hand back a shovel-ready site — cleared, cut to grade, piped and compacted — with our own equipment and crews.

Site Clearing & Demolition

Clearing, grubbing and demolition that turns an overgrown or built-up parcel into open, workable ground.

Excavation & Earthwork

Mass and structural excavation, cut-and-fill, hauling and export — moved with our own heavy fleet.

Grading & Site Preparation

Rough and fine grading, fill placement and compaction to engineered elevations, ready for foundations and paving.

Underground Utilities

Water, sanitary sewer and storm systems installed to spec — certified, statewide, by a CUC-licensed contractor.

Concrete & Flatwork

Curb, gutter, sidewalks, pads and site flatwork placed and finished as part of a complete site package.

Paving & Drainage

Sub-base preparation and paving support, plus storm and drainage structures that keep a finished site dry.

A family firm since 1987

Three generations of Bradys, one standard on the dirt.

Brady Construction of Ocala has been a family-run, Ocala-based contractor since 1987. The name on the door is the name that answers the phone — David Brady and family still run the crews, price the work, and stand behind it.

We hold a certified underground utility and excavation license good across the state of Florida, and we self-perform the heavy work rather than sub it out. That is how a site gets done right, on grade, and on schedule.
