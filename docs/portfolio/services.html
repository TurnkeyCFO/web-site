Frequently asked questions.

How fast is the 48-hour delivery?+
From the moment you approve the brief and make the initial payment, the clock starts. Most projects are complete — live, deployed, fully functional — within 48 hours. Complex custom builds may take 72–96 hours, quoted upfront.
Do I need to provide content and copy?+
Ideally yes — you know your business better than I do. But I can write placeholder copy and structure content that you refine after delivery. For landing pages especially, I'll draft conversion-focused copy based on your brief.
What if I want changes after delivery?+
Every package includes at least 2 full rounds of revisions. After that, additional revision rounds are available at a flat hourly rate. The Business and Custom packages also include 30–60 days of post-launch support.
Can I host the site myself?+
Absolutely — you receive the complete source code and can host it anywhere. I typically recommend GitHub Pages (free) or Netlify (free tier). I'll help you deploy to whichever platform you prefer, or handle it entirely if you want.
Do you do ongoing maintenance?+
Yes. After the included support period ends, I offer monthly maintenance retainers starting at $150/month for content updates, edits, and additions. Larger ongoing projects are scoped individually.
How do I get started?+
Send me an email at Ricky@turnkeyweb.com with a brief description of your project — what you need, examples you like, and your timeline. I'll respond with a quote and project brief within a few hours.
