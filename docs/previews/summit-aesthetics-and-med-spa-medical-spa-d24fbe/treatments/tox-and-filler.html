What We Treat

Frown & Forehead Lines

Thin or Uneven Lips

Common Questions

Injectables, answered.

Most clients describe injectable treatments as a quick pinch. We use fine needles, and filler products contain a numbing agent. Topical numbing is available for sensitive areas, and your injector will keep you comfortable throughout.

Neuromodulator results typically last three to four months. Dermal filler lasts roughly 9 to 18 months depending on the product and treatment area. Your injector will give you a realistic timeline at your consultation.

Not at Summit. Our house style is restraint — anatomy-led, conservative dosing, and results that look rested rather than altered. Our injectors are known for talking clients out of more, not into it.

Most clients return to normal activities the same day. Minor swelling or bruising can occur, particularly with filler, and usually settles within a few days. Your injector will review simple aftercare with you.
