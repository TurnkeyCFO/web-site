Meet the owner

Hi, I'm Marcus — and I'll be the one detailing your vehicle.

I started Red Oak Mobile Detailing for a simple reason: I love the moment a vehicle goes from tired and overlooked to genuinely like-new — and I wanted to do that work the right way, on my own terms.

The name comes from the red oaks all over Central Texas — steady, rooted, and built to last. That's the standard I hold every detail to. When you book Red Oak, you're not handing your car to whoever's on shift. You're booking me, and I treat every vehicle like it's parked in my own driveway.

Because I'm a one-man operation, I keep things deliberately simple: clear packages, honest prices you can see before you book, and a service that comes to you. No franchise overhead, no upsell games — just careful, professional detailing at your home or office.

Why mobile, why this way

Built to remove the hassle — not add to it.

Most people put off detailing because of everything around it: the drive, the wait, the texting back and forth for a price. Red Oak was designed to cut all of that out.

  • I come to you

    Your driveway, apartment lot or office parking spot becomes the shop. You keep your day.

  • Prices are public

    Every package and add-on is listed on the site. You never have to message me to find out what something costs.

  • Booking is self-serve

    Pick a package and a time online whenever it suits you — even at 11pm. I confirm and we're set.

  • The work is unrushed

    One vehicle at a time, given the hours it actually needs. No assembly-line pace.

What Red Oak stands for

The values behind every detail.

Craftsmanship

Detailing is patient work. I'd rather take an extra half hour than leave a job that's only most of the way there.

Straight talk

If a package isn't right for your vehicle, I'll tell you. Honest advice keeps customers coming back.

Respect for your time

On-time arrival, a clear finish window, and a service that fits around your schedule — not the other way around.

Care for the vehicle

Safe products and gentle technique. The goal is to protect your paint and interior, not just shine them once.

A real relationship

The same detailer every time means I learn your vehicle — and you always know who you're dealing with.

Pride in the result

I don't leave a driveway until the vehicle is something I'd be glad to put the Red Oak name on.
