006 Frequently asked

Questions before you start.

Wait — it's really free? What's the catch?+
No catch. We design your first complete draft at zero cost so you can see real work for your actual business before deciding anything. There's no card required and no obligation. If you love it, you keep it live for a flat $147/month — hosting, unlimited edits, and support all included. If you don't, you walk away owing nothing.
How is this different from Wix or Squarespace?+
DIY platforms hand you a blank canvas and a steep learning curve. Most owners end up with a site that looks like a template — and converts like one. We build a custom site designed around how your customers actually decide to call you, then maintain it for you. You stay focused on the business; we handle the website.
What's included in the $147/month?+
Everything to keep the site live and working: fast managed hosting and SSL, unlimited edits (text, photos, new pages — no per-change fees), domain setup help, an on-page SEO foundation (titles, meta, schema, sitemap, Google Business Profile alignment), and ongoing support and updates.
What if I don't love the first draft?+
That's what unlimited revisions are for. We refine — swap sections, change copy, rework the design — until you genuinely love it. And since you only pay once you decide to go live, there's zero risk in seeing it first.
Do I own the website?+
Yes. While you're on the monthly plan the site is fully yours — your content, your domain, your brand. We don't hold anything hostage, and there are no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime.
Is there really no build fee or contract?+
Correct. No upfront build fee, no setup fee, no annual contract. You see the draft free and, if you keep it, it's a simple month-to-month $147 plan you can cancel whenever you want.
I'm not in Austin — can you still work with me?+
Yes. We're based in Austin, TX but build sites for businesses anywhere in the U.S. The whole process is remote — a short form, async reviews, and your draft delivered by email. Most clients never need a call.
Will my site rank on Google?+
Every site ships with strong on-page SEO foundations — proper titles, meta tags, schema markup, fast load times, mobile-first layout, and a clean sitemap. That makes you eligible to rank; sustained ranking for competitive terms takes ongoing content work, which we can discuss once you're live.
