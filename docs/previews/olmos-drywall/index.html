What Miguel fixes

Every kind of drywall trouble — handled.

Drywall repair

Holes, dents, cracks, and door-knob dings — cut out, patched, sanded, and blended into the surrounding wall.

Texture matching

Knockdown, orange peel, popcorn, hand-trowel — the repair is retextured to match your existing wall so it vanishes.

Water-damage restoration

Stained, sagging, or failed ceilings and walls from leaks — cut back to solid, rebuilt, textured, and made new.

Patch & patchwork

Access holes from plumbing or electrical, remodel cutouts, and settling cracks — patched flush and finished ready for paint.

Nail-pop & screw fixing

Popped nails and screws that dimple your walls — reset, refastened, filled, and smoothed so the surface reads flat again.

Wallpaper removal & retexture

Old wallpaper stripped, walls skimmed and retextured, and left smooth and primed — ready for your painter.

Matched so well you can't tell

Three steps to a wall that hides the past.

  1. 01

    Assess & cut to solid

    Find the real edge of the damage, remove what's failed, and set a clean, sound base to build back from.

  2. 02

    Patch, tape & float

    Rebuild the surface, tape the seams, and float the compound wide so the repair sits perfectly flat.

  3. 03

    Texture-match & finish

    Retexture to match the wall exactly and leave it primed and ready — so all you see is the wall, not the fix.
