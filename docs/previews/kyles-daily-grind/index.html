Our Story

Genuine connections,
one cup at a time.

Kyle's Daily Grind started with a simple idea: make the kind of coffee shop you actually want to linger in. Millie and her crew know the regulars by name, remember the usual, and treat every drink like it matters — because it does.

Inside it's warm and easy — a few chairs, good light, the smell of something baking. Outside, a drive-thru for the mornings you're already running late. Either way, you're getting a drink made by hand and a hello that means it.
