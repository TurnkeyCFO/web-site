Hair Salon FAQ

Questions salons ask us first.

I already have Instagram. Do I still need a salon website?

Instagram is where clients browse — but a website is where they book. Instagram can't take appointments, can't rank in Google Search for "hair salon near me," and sends your audience to a platform you don't own. Your website ties the beautiful work on Instagram to a seamless booking experience you fully control.

What's the most important feature for a hair salon website?

Online booking — by a wide margin. A client who has to call during salon hours to book an appointment will often book a competitor instead. An online booking integration lets clients schedule at midnight on their phone while scrolling your gallery. That's when salons fill their chairs.

Can my website showcase individual stylists and their work?

Yes, and it should. Clients often choose a salon based on a specific stylist whose work they've seen. A stylist showcase — with photos, specialties, and a direct book button — lets clients connect with the right person before they ever walk in, and it keeps your chairs full across the whole team.

Will my salon show up when people search "hair salon near me"?

Every site we build ships SEO-ready: proper title tags, local-business schema, service-area pages, and fast mobile load — the foundations Google rewards. Paired with your Google Business Profile, it gives your salon a real shot at the local map pack for "hair salon near me" and specific service searches like "balayage Austin."

How fast can my hair salon website go live?

Your first complete draft lands within 7 days of your brand questionnaire. Most hair salon sites are fully live within 2 to 3 weeks.

What does the $250 setup cover?

Everything to launch: design, build, copywriting layout, booking integration, gallery setup, stylist showcase, mobile optimization, on-page SEO, hosting setup, SSL, domain connection, and unlimited revisions until you love it. Same $250 whether it's one page or twenty. Then $50/mo covers hosting, management, and unlimited updates.

Do I have to write my own copy or supply all the photos?

No. You answer a short questionnaire about your services, stylists, ambiance, pricing, and booking tool — we handle the writing and design. You supply the gallery photos (your Instagram is usually a great source), and we build the rest.
