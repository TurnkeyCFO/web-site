Despre noi · Our Church

Rooted in the Word,
gathered as o familie.

Bethany is a Romanian-speaking Pentecostal congregation in Abbotsford, set in the heart of British Columbia's Fraser Valley. Week by week, families come together to worship, to pray, and to open the Scriptures — in the language that still feels most like home.

From children's classes and a youth orchestra to outreach across the Lower Mainland, the life of our church reaches every generation. We believe the Gospel is good news for the whole family — and our door is always open to anyone who wants to come and see.
