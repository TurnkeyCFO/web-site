Most small business owners already know their website is holding them back. It loads slowly, it looks dated, it is hard to update, and it does not send them a single new lead. So it stays on the to-do list for another year while the phone stays quieter than it should.

Turnkey Web is here to take that problem off your plate. We are the team that designs and builds a modern, fast, mobile-first site for your business, then keeps it running so you never have to think about it again. You stay focused on the work you are great at. We handle the website.

How we work

We build fast, and we build on systems. Instead of dragging a project out over months of back-and-forth, we gather what we need up front, get a real draft in front of you quickly, and refine it with you until it is right. Every site is designed to look credible, load quickly on a phone, and point visitors toward the one thing that matters to you, whether that is a call, a booking, or a filled-out form.

Because we run on a repeatable process rather than reinventing the wheel each time, you get agency-quality work without the agency wait or the agency runaround.

Who is behind it

Founder

Ricky West

Ricky founded Turnkey Web in Austin, Texas after seeing the same story again and again: hard-working owners paying for websites that never earned their keep. He built Turnkey Web to close that gap with a simple promise, that a small business should be able to get a website that looks the part and pulls its weight, without the mystery, the markup, or the maintenance headaches.

He leads every build with the same standard he would want as an owner himself: clear communication, honest advice, and a finished site you are proud to send people to.
