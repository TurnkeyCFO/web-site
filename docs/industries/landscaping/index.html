The landscaping reality

Why a real website wins for landscapers specifically.

Landscaping is bought with the eyes. The business model runs on recurring accounts. Neither works without a website built for how customers actually decide.

Homeowners buy with their eyes first

The portfolio is the pitch

Nobody hires a landscaper without picturing what their yard could look like. If your best transformations aren't online, you're asking homeowners to imagine — and they'll hire the competitor whose photos they can actually see.

Recurring revenue is the real prize

Maintenance accounts are worth far more than installs

A weekly mowing client or annual maintenance plan compounds for years. A website with a dedicated recurring-service section converts one-time inquiries into ongoing accounts that stabilize your revenue through slow seasons.

Seasonal surges reward who's visible

Spring and fall are won in search

Texas homeowners make landscaping decisions in bursts — spring planting, summer irrigation, fall cleanup. The landscaper with a fast, visible website captures that surge of intent. Everyone else gets the leftovers.

Curb appeal work is neighborhood-contagious

One stunning yard generates the next lead

Neighbors notice a great job. If your website shows it — with project photos, a service area, and a clear way to reach you — that curb-appeal envy becomes a qualified inquiry instead of "I wonder who did that."

Landscaping FAQ

Questions landscapers ask us first.

I get most of my landscaping clients through referrals. Do I really need a website?

Referrals are great — but your referred lead will Google you before they ever call. With no website, or a weak one, that warm lead quietly hires the competitor who showed up first. A professional site with your best project photos confirms you're the real deal and captures the homeowners referrals never reach.

My landscaping work is visual. Can a website actually show that?

That's exactly the point. A well-built landscaping website is a curated photo gallery — before-and-after sliders, project grids, full-bleed outdoor photography — that sells the work better than any sales pitch. We design every page to put your best projects front and center, on every screen.

How do I get recurring maintenance clients, not just one-time jobs?

We build a dedicated recurring-services section that makes your lawn care plans, weekly mowing, and seasonal programs easy to find and easy to sign up for. A clear service menu with a simple contact form converts one-time browsers into ongoing accounts.

Will my landscaping website show up when people search locally?

Every site we build ships SEO-ready: proper title tags, local-business schema, service-area pages for your target neighborhoods, and fast mobile load times — the technical foundations Google rewards. Paired with your Google Business Profile, you have a real shot at the local map results for "landscaper near me" and "lawn care [your city]."

How fast can my landscaping website go live?

Your first complete draft lands within 7 days of your brand questionnaire. Most landscaping sites are fully live within 2 to 3 weeks.

What does the $250 setup fee cover?

Everything to launch: design, build, photo layout, copywriting, mobile optimization, on-page SEO, hosting setup, SSL, domain connection, and unlimited revisions until you love it. Same $250 whether it's one page or twenty. Then $50/mo covers hosting, management, and unlimited updates going forward.

Do I need to provide professional photos?

Phone photos of your real projects work great — they're authentic and customers trust them. We lay them out to look professional. You answer a short questionnaire about your services, service area, and the projects you're most proud of; we handle the writing, layout, and design.
