What we fix

One call for every appliance in the house.

If it heats, cools, spins, or washes, Nick and the ASAP crew have seen it — and fixed it. Straight diagnosis, honest pricing, no run-around.

Refrigerators & Freezers

Not cooling, leaking, or icing over? We repair compressors, seals, thermostats and defrost systems to keep your food safe.

Washers

Won't drain, won't spin, or walking across the floor? We fix pumps, belts, bearings and control boards on top- and front-loaders.

Dryers

No heat, no tumble, or taking three cycles to dry? We repair heating elements, thermostats and drums — including dryers that won't spin.

Ranges & Ovens

Electric ranges that won't heat, uneven baking, dead burners or bad igniters — repaired so dinner's back on schedule.

Dishwashers

Not draining, not cleaning, or leaking at the door? We service pumps, spray arms, seals and control panels.

All Major Appliances

Every major brand, most makes and models. If it plugs in and stopped working, call us first — chances are we can fix it.
