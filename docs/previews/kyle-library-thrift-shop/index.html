Good to know

Questions, answered

Where is the Kyle Library Thrift Shop located?+

We're at 100 W. Lockhart St. in downtown Kyle, TX 78640 — minutes from Buda and San Marcos, just off the I-35 corridor.

What are your store hours?+

We're open Monday through Saturday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., and closed on Sundays. Holiday hours are posted in-store and on our Facebook page.

What kinds of items do you sell?+

Furniture, clothing, books, household goods, home decor, antiques, electronics and sporting equipment — all at bargain thrift-store prices, with new arrivals every day.

How do I donate items?+

Bring your gently used goods to the shop during open hours and a team member will help you unload. For large furniture, give us a quick call first at (512) 268-1036.

Where does the money go?+

We're operated by The Friends of the Kyle Library. Every item donated and purchased goes directly back into programs and resources at the Kyle Public Library.
