Frequently asked

Questions before you book.

Everything most small business owners ask before working with us — straight answers, no fluff.

How is this different from Wix, Squarespace, or building it myself?

DIY platforms hand you a blank canvas and a steep learning curve. Most owners end up with a site that looks like a template — and converts like one. We build a custom site designed around how your customers actually decide to call you, with unlimited revisions until it's right. You stay focused on running the business; we handle the website.

What does the 3-Day Guarantee actually mean?

Once we have your assets from the brand questionnaire, your first complete draft is live for review within 3 days. If we miss that window for any reason on our end, you don't pay. Simple as that.

The clock starts when your questionnaire is complete — not when you book the call.

What if I don't love the first draft?

That's what unlimited revisions are for. We refine — swap sections, change copy, rework the design — until you genuinely love it. We don't launch a site you're not proud of.

Do I own the website when it's done?

Yes. Full ownership of the code, the content, the domain, and the hosting account. We don't hold anything hostage. If you ever decide to part ways, the entire site goes with you.

What's included? Hosting? SEO? Edits after launch?

Included in every build: custom design, unlimited pages, mobile-first responsive layout, fast hosting setup, on-page SEO foundation (titles, meta, schema, sitemap), Google Business Profile alignment, and a clean handoff.

After launch: ongoing edits and updates are part of our monthly plan — text changes, new sections, new pages, photo swaps, whatever you need. No "change request" fees, no per-edit billing.

How much does it cost?

Build pricing is set per project based on scope and complexity — most service-business sites land between $2,000 and $6,000 for the initial build, with a flat monthly plan for ongoing updates and hosting after launch.

On the 15-minute call we'll quote you an exact number for your project before you commit to anything.

I'm not in Austin — can you still work with me?

Yes. We're based in Austin, TX but we build sites for businesses anywhere in the U.S. The entire process is remote — short calls, a questionnaire, async reviews. Most clients never need to meet in person.

Will my site rank on Google?

Every site is built with strong on-page SEO foundations — proper titles, meta tags, schema markup, fast load times, mobile-first, and a clean sitemap submitted to Google Search Console. That gets you eligible to rank.

Sustained ranking (especially for competitive keywords) takes ongoing content and link work. We offer SEO as a separate service if you want to push beyond the foundation.
