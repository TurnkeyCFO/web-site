You-Pick Field

Come Out
& Cut Your Own

A few mornings each season, the rows open for "come and cut." Wander the field, fill your basket, and gather your own bouquet under the wide open Texas sky. It's part flower-shopping, part golden-hour wander — bring a friend, bring your camera, take your time.

Dates and availability shift with the bloom calendar. Send a note and I'll let you know when the next field days open up.
