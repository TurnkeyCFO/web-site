The Craft

Two decades of
reading hair right.

SoQ Salon isn't a chain and it never will be. It's one veteran stylist who has spent 25-plus years learning exactly how color lifts, how a cut falls, and how to fix the mess someone else left behind.

People drive across Houston for it. Clients have been in this chair for fifteen years, and their kids sit in it now too. That's the whole story: get it right, be honest, and treat every head of hair like it matters.
