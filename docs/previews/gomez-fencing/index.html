What We Build

Every fence, built to hold the line.

New builds, custom layouts, or a tired old fence that needs to go — we handle it all, and we tell you straight what your yard needs before we start.

Wood & Cedar Fences

Cedar pickets, treated pine, and custom wood layouts set on solid posts. Clean lines that stand up to Gulf-Coast heat, rain, and wind for years.

Privacy Fence Installation

Full-height privacy fencing with tight, gap-free boards — keep the neighbors out of your business and your backyard yours. Custom heights and cap-and-trim options available.

Pool & Dog Fences

Code-minded pool fencing and secure dog-run enclosures. Keep kids and pets safe with fencing built to the right height and spacing for your property.

Rail Fences & Custom Fabrication

2-rail and 3-rail ranch fencing, plus custom fabrication and fence design for layouts that aren't off-the-shelf. Tell us the vision — we'll build it.

Repairs & Rotting-Fence Replacement

Leaning posts, storm damage, rotted pickets, sagging gates — we repair what's worth saving and replace what isn't. Honest assessment first, every time.

Backyard Decking

Custom wood decking to match your new fence and finish the yard. One crew, one clean look, from the deck boards to the back gate.
