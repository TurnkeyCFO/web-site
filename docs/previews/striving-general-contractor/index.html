What we do

One crew, start to finish.

Striving is a general contractor built around painting and remodeling. The same people who prep and paint are the ones setting your trim, laying your floor and cleaning up at the end of the day. No hand-offs, no finger-pointing.

01 Interior painting Walls, ceilings, trim and doors. Proper prep, clean lines, and drop cloths over everything you own.
02 Exterior & facade painting A fresh, honest face on the house. Scrape, prime, caulk and coat, so it still looks right in five years.
03 Kitchen & bathroom remodeling The two rooms that decide how a house feels. Cabinets, tile, fixtures and finish work.
04 Basement finishing Turning square footage you already pay for into a room the family actually uses.
05 Flooring Hardwood, laminate, vinyl plank and tile, laid level and finished tight to the trim.
06 Siding & windows Replace what the Missouri weather has been working on, and seal the house back up.
07 Decks & porches Build it, rebuild it, or bring the one you have back from the weather.
