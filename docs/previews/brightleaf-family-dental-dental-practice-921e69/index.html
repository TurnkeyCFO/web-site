Good to know

Answers to the questions new patients ask first.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes. Brightleaf Family Dental is actively welcoming new patients of all ages from Franklin, Brentwood, and the surrounding Williamson County area. You can request a visit through our appointment form or by calling (615) 770-3344.

Do you take my insurance?

We accept most major dental plans, including Delta Dental, Cigna, MetLife, Aetna, Guardian, BlueCross BlueShield, and United Concordia. When you request a visit, we'll verify your specific benefits before you arrive so there are no surprises.

What if I have a dental emergency?

We hold same-day emergency appointments open every day. If you're in pain, have a broken or knocked-out tooth, or lost a crown or filling, call us as early as you can and our front desk will triage your situation and get you seen.
