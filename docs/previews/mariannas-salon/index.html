The Work

A short, serious menu —
done beautifully.

01 — Hair Color

Color that looks like you

Single-process, gloss, gray coverage, full transformation — color is the heart of the studio. We take the time to get the tone right, so it grows out soft and reads natural in every light.

02 — Balayage & Highlights

Sun-kissed, hand-painted dimension

Soft balayage, bright foil highlights, lived-in blends — placed by hand and toned to flatter your skin. Low-maintenance by design, so you're not chasing a root line every three weeks.

03 — Blowouts & Cuts

A blowout you can recreate

Smooth, full, photo-ready blowouts for an event — or a fresh shape and finish that holds all week. Cuts and styling that work with your hair instead of fighting it.
