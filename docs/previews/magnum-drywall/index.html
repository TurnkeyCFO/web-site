The finish standard · GA-214

Five levels of finish.
We nail the one you need.

Not every wall needs a mirror finish — and paying for one you don't need is wasted budget. We finish to the industry level your space actually calls for, and we tell you which that is up front.

0Rough-in

No finish required

Board hung, no tape or compound — temporary construction or areas that'll be concealed.

1–2Utility

Garages & storage

Tape embedded, one coat over joints and fasteners — solid where appearance is secondary.

3Standard

Under heavy texture

Two coats over tape and heads — the base for medium-to-heavy sprayed or hand texture.

4Flat & light texture

Most homes

Three coats, sanded smooth — ideal for flat paints and light textures in living spaces.

5Our specialty

Glass-smooth, gloss-ready

A full skim coat over the entire surface — flawless under gloss, enamel, and raking light. The premium finish.

Not sure which you need?

Tell us the space and the paint. We'll spec the right level and price it honestly.
