Home / FAQ

Questions, answered honestly.

The things clients ask us most — about consultations, safety, pricing, membership, and what your first visit really looks like.

Yes. Every new client begins with a consultation so a clinician can review your goals, health history, and anatomy before recommending anything. For many treatments the consultation and treatment happen in the same visit; some advanced services are planned for a later appointment.

Yes. Every Summit location operates under physician supervision. Our medical director reviews the clinical protocols behind every treatment we offer, and treatments are delivered by credentialed, master-certified injectors and clinicians.

Absolutely. Summit shares one client record across all four locations, so your chart, treatment history, and Summit Society credits move with you whether you visit Scottsdale, Phoenix, Gilbert, or Chandler.

Starting prices are published on our pricing page. Because aesthetic medicine is individual, your exact cost is confirmed in writing at your consultation, based on your plan and the product quantity used. Summit Society members receive 10–15% off most treatments.

Yes. We accept major cards and offer financing through trusted third-party providers for larger treatment plans. Pre-paid packages and the Summit Society membership are also popular ways to spread out the cost of regular care.

You can reschedule or cancel from the client portal or by calling your location. We ask for at least 24 hours' notice so we can offer the time to another client; details of our cancellation policy are confirmed when you book.

When performed by trained, credentialed clinicians under physician supervision, injectable treatments have a well-established safety profile. Every plan begins with a health screening, and your injector will review benefits, risks, and aftercare with you before treating.

Membership is month-to-month with no contract. Your monthly fee converts into a treatment credit that rolls over for up to 12 months and can be spent on any treatment or retail product at any location. Members also receive standing discounts and early access. You can pause or cancel anytime and keep credits you have already earned.

Plan for an unhurried consultation. You will meet your clinician, discuss your goals and history, and receive an honest assessment and a written plan. There is never pressure to book a treatment on the day — many clients leave to think it over and return when they're ready.
