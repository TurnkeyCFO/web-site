Tax time shouldn’t feel like a second job.

For more than a decade, One Stop Taxes has sat right on Fort Campbell Boulevard, filing returns for soldiers, families, and small-business owners across Clarksville. You bring your paperwork — we handle the rest, explain it in plain English, and make sure every credit and deduction you’ve earned lands in your refund.

No hidden fees, no jargon, no runaround. Just careful, certified work from people who’ll still be here next April.

What we do

Everything you need, under one roof.

From a simple W-2 to a full business return, it’s all handled here — in the office or fully online.

Individual Tax Prep

W-2s, 1099s, itemized returns, dependents, and every credit you qualify for — filed accurately and on time.

Business & Self-Employed

Schedule C, LLC, partnership, and corporate returns — plus the year-round guidance to keep your books clean.

Fast E-File & Refunds

IRS/EFILE-certified electronic filing so your return goes out the door and your refund comes back as quickly as possible.

Tax Planning

A plan for next year, not just this one — withholding, estimated payments, and moves to lower what you owe.

IRS Resolution & Audits

Notices, back taxes, and audit support — we’ll stand between you and the IRS and get it sorted.

Notary & Virtual Filing

On-site notary services and secure remote filing — do the whole thing from your couch if you’d rather not drive.

How it works

Three steps. That’s the whole thing.

01

Bring your paperwork

Walk in, call ahead, or upload securely from home. W-2s, 1099s, last year’s return — whatever you’ve got.

02

A certified preparer builds your return by hand, chases down every credit, and walks you through it line by line.

03

We e-file the moment you approve it, then track your refund all the way to your account.
