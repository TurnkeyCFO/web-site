The Summit Society.

A membership built for the clients who make Summit part of their routine — rewarding loyalty with real treatment credits, member pricing, and early access. No contracts, ever.

How It Works

Your membership pays you back.

Every month, your fee becomes a credit. Credits roll over, stack, and can be spent on any treatment or retail product at any location.

01

Choose a tier

Pick the membership that matches how often you treat. You can move between tiers anytime as your routine changes.

02

Earn monthly credits

Your fee converts to a treatment credit each month. Unused credits roll over for up to 12 months — they never expire on a technicality.

03

Spend it your way

Apply credits to injectables, laser, facials, body contouring, IV therapy, or retail skincare — at whichever studio you visit.

Choose Your Tier

Two memberships. Zero contracts.

Both tiers are month-to-month. Pause or cancel anytime — any credits you have already earned remain yours to spend.
