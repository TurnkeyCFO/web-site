What we treat

From a nagging heel to
reconstructive surgery.

Nearly every foot and ankle concern is handled right here in the office — the same doctors, start to finish. If it touches the foot, ankle or lower leg, it belongs with us.

Heel Pain & Plantar Fasciitis

That first painful step in the morning. We find the cause and calm it — including EPAT shockwave for stubborn cases.

Bunions & Hammertoes

Relief first, surgery only when it's truly the answer — with modern forefoot correction when you need it.

Diabetic Foot Care

Careful, regular attention that protects circulation and sensation — and keeps small problems from becoming big ones.

Ingrown & Fungal Nails

Quick, gentle in-office treatment for painful ingrown nails, thickened nails and stubborn fungus.

Sports & Ankle Injuries

Sprains, instability, fractures and Achilles pain — imaged on-site and treated to get you moving again.

Wounds, Warts & Everyday Care

Ulcers and wound care, warts, calluses, athlete's foot, flat feet, neuropathy and arthritis — all under one roof.

Not sure what's causing the pain? Call and we'll help you figure it out.

Meet your doctors

Two doctors. One family.

The name on the door is the doctor you'll see. A father-and-son practice that has cared for Ocala's feet for a generation — down-to-earth, unrushed, and genuinely invested in getting you comfortable again.

Dr. Bruce Wolosky, DPM

Founder · Foot & Ankle Physician & Surgeon

A University of Miami and Barry University graduate with more than 30 years in practice, Dr. Wolosky opened Podiatry Services of Florida in 1998 after surgical training in West Palm Beach. Patients describe him the same way again and again: honest, funny, and someone who truly listens.

  • DPM, Barry University School of Podiatric Medicine
  • Surgical residency, Wellington Regional (West Palm Beach)
  • Serving the Ocala community since 1996

Dr. Michael Wolosky, DPM, AACFAS

Foot & Ankle Surgeon

Bruce's son and surgical partner, Dr. Michael Wolosky completed a foot-and-ankle surgical residency at Jefferson Health in Philadelphia. He focuses on lower-extremity trauma, nerve surgery, reconstruction and limb salvage — bringing advanced surgical care home to Ocala.

  • DPM, Barry University · AACFAS board-qualified
  • Surgical residency, Jefferson Health Northeast
  • Trauma, nerve surgery & reconstruction
