Our practice

Eleven years of caring for Franklin smiles.

Brightleaf Family Dental was built on a simple idea: a dental visit should be calm, honest, and easy to fit into a real life. Here's who we are and how we work.

Our story

A neighborhood practice, grown the right way.

Brightleaf opened its doors in Franklin in 2015 with two dentists, a handful of operatories, and a commitment to do dentistry differently.

Over eleven years we've grown into a team of nine — hygienists, assistants, and patient coordinators — but the goal has never changed. We wanted a practice where families could get every kind of care in one place, where appointments started on time, and where treatment was explained, not sold.

Today, Brightleaf is a full family and cosmetic dental home for patients across Franklin, Brentwood, and Williamson County. From a toddler's first cleaning to an adult's complete smile makeover, it all happens under one roof — with the same calm, modern feeling you'd want for your own family.

Meet your dentist

Care led by someone who knows your name.

Brightleaf is a two-dentist practice, which is exactly why families can be seen together. Our practice is led by Dr. Priya Anand.

Lead Dentist · General & Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Priya Anand

Dr. Anand leads the clinical team at Brightleaf Family Dental, where she has helped build a practice known for unhurried, honest care. She is happiest doing the everyday dentistry that keeps families healthy — thorough cleanings, careful exams, and cosmetic work that looks natural rather than overdone.

Her approach is simple: explain clearly, never pressure, and treat every patient the way she'd want her own family treated. New patients often mention how different a no-rush, no-sales-pitch visit feels.

Rooted in Franklin

Proud to be your hometown dental office.

We're not a chain and we're not a revolving door. Brightleaf is a single, independent practice in Franklin — and that's exactly how we like it.

Many of our patients found us when they were new to Williamson County and looking for a family dentist they could simply trust. Eleven years in, we've watched kids grow from their first cleaning into their teen years, and welcomed their parents and grandparents too.

Being a neighborhood practice means we answer to one group of people: our patients. That's the standard we hold every visit to.
