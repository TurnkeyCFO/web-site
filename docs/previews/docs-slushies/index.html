FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

Do you serve alcoholic frozen drinks? +

We can build a 21+ spiked menu (frozen margaritas, sangria, and more) or keep it completely family-friendly with lemonades and fruit slushes — or run both side by side. Tell us your event and we'll walk you through the right setup.

What areas do you serve? +

We're based in College Station and serve Bryan–College Station and the surrounding Brazos Valley. Traveling a bit farther for the right event? Just ask — we'll let you know.

How much power and space do you need? +

Our station fits a standard tailgate lot, backyard, or venue corner. We'll confirm power needs when we scope your event — and for spots without easy power, we'll plan around it.

How many people can one machine serve? +

It depends on headcount and how long you want the taps flowing. We size the number of machines and flavors to your guest count so the line keeps moving the whole time.

How far ahead should I book? +

The sooner the better — game-day weekends and wedding season fill up fast in Aggieland. Send your date and we'll confirm availability and hold it for you.

Do you handle setup and cleanup? +

Always. We arrive early, set up, pour and restock through your event, then break down and pack out clean. You don't lift a finger.
