Home / Our Story

Aesthetic medicine, done with intention.

Summit Aesthetics & Med Spa exists to prove that clinical credibility and a genuine sense of escape are not a trade-off — you should have both.

How Summit Began

Founded on a better standard of care.

Summit was founded by Alexandra Whitmore after years of watching aesthetic medicine pull in two directions — clinics that felt sterile and rushed, and spas that felt lovely but light on medical rigour.

She believed clients deserved a third option: a place with the oversight of a medical practice and the warmth of a spa, where the same standard of care held true at every location and every appointment. Summit grew from a single Scottsdale studio into four Valley locations — and that founding belief has never been diluted to scale.

Today, every Summit location is physician-supervised, every injector is master-certified, and every plan is built around the person in the chair — not a treatment quota.

What We Believe

Four principles behind every appointment.

Medicine First

Aesthetic treatments are medical procedures. Physician oversight and proper protocols come before anything else.

Honesty Always

If a treatment won't serve you, we say so. Our injectors are known for talking clients out of things, not into them.

Natural Results

The Summit house style is restraint. You should look rested and like yourself — never altered or overdone.

Consistency

One standard, four locations. Your experience should not change because your week did.

Built for the Valley

Care that meets you where you live.

Summit operates four locations — Scottsdale, Phoenix, Gilbert, and Chandler — so the Valley's best aesthetic care is never a long drive away.

Our Gilbert location is undergoing a full refresh and celebrates its grand reopening on October 1st, with an expanded laser suite and a dedicated IV therapy lounge. Wherever you choose to be seen, your chart, your preferences, and your treatment plan follow you.
