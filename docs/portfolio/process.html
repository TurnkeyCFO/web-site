The exact steps, from start to finish.

Every project follows the same proven sequence. Here's what happens from the moment you reach out.

01
Hours 0–2
Initial Brief & Scope

You send me an email describing what you need — pages, features, any examples you like. I reply within a few hours with a clear scope, fixed price, and project brief for your approval.

→Email Ricky@turnkeyweb.com with your project overview
→I reply with a fixed-price quote and project scope document
→You approve scope and make initial payment — work begins immediately
02
Hours 2–24
Design & Build

I build your design system first — colors, typography, component language — then build out every page or section. No separate wireframe phase. You see real, working HTML within the first day.

→Custom design system built from scratch for your brand
→Hand-coded HTML/CSS — no page builders, no WordPress
→Preview link sent within 24 hours so you can see real progress
03
Hours 24–40
Review & Revisions

You review the live preview and send feedback. I apply all revisions same-day. Two full rounds of revisions are included in every package — most projects finish in one round.

→Preview delivered via GitHub Pages link — works on any device
→Revisions applied within hours of receiving your feedback
→2 full rounds of revisions included — no hourly charges
04
Hours 40–48
Deploy & Handoff

Once approved, I deploy the site to your domain, configure DNS and SSL, and send you a handoff package with the source code, deployment documentation, and access credentials. You're live.

→DNS configuration and SSL certificate setup handled
→Full source code delivered — you own it forever
→30 days of included post-launch support starts on go-live

What to expect working with me.

Not promises — specifics. Here's what every client experiences, every time.

Communication
Same-day responses, always.
Every message is answered the same business day. No waiting days for a reply to a simple question. You're not managed — you're a priority.
Transparency
No surprises on scope or price.
What's in the quote is what you pay. If scope grows, I'll flag it and requote before proceeding — never after the fact.
Ownership
You own everything I build.
Source code, design files, deployment configs — all delivered at handoff. No subscriptions, no platform lock-in, no recurring fees to me.
Quality
Production-grade code, not demo code.
Every site is built to the standard I'd want for my own business — clean structure, semantic HTML, no dead links, no lorem ipsum in production.
Speed

No external frameworks unless necessary, no unoptimized images, no render-blocking scripts. Fast by default, not by optimization after the fact.
Support
30 days after launch, included.
Text changes, image swaps, link fixes — all covered in the post-launch window. If something breaks, I fix it same-day, no questions asked.
